Background & Historical Context

To understand why Zelensky is pushing for a seat at the table now, we have to look at why previous attempts failed.

Pre‑2022 Diplomatic Efforts

  • Minsk Agreements (2015) – A failed attempt to stop the Donbas fighting that crumbled under constant violations.
  • NATO‑Ukraine Partnership – Closer military ties that Moscow viewed as a direct security threat.
  • EU Association Agreement (2014) – Kyiv’s pivot West, which triggered a fierce Russian response.

These failures explain the current strategy. Zelensky wants to capture the intent of the Minsk spirit but strip away its flaws.

Recent Escalations

  1. 2023‑2024 Frontline Shifts – A brutal tug-of-war where Russia seized strategic towns while Ukraine reclaimed vital supply lines.

This volatility created the perfect storm for a bold diplomatic move.
