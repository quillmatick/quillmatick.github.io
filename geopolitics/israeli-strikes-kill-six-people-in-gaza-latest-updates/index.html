Tragedies like this don't happen in a vacuum. They are the result of decades of failed treaties and recurring escalations.

  • 2008‑2009 Gaza War (Operation Cast Lead)

    • Left over 1,400 Palestinians dead, many of them civilians.

Post‑2020 Escalations

  • 2021 May Conflict
